Frequently Asked Questions

Is a self-generated salary slip valid for bank loans? ▼
Salary slips generated by employers (including small businesses using tools like this) are generally accepted by banks for personal loans and credit cards. However, for home loans, banks may require salary slips along with Form 16 or ITR for stronger verification. Always check with your specific bank's requirements.
What is PF and is it mandatory? ▼
Provident Fund (PF) is mandatory for companies with 20 or more employees where the employee's salary is below ₹15,000/month. For higher salaries or smaller companies, it is optional. Employee contributes 12% of Basic Salary, and the employer matches it. You can toggle PF off if it doesn't apply to your case.
What is Professional Tax? ▼
Professional Tax (PT) is a state-level tax deducted from employees. The amount varies by state — Maharashtra charges ₹200/month for salaries above ₹10,000. Not all states have PT (e.g., Rajasthan, Delhi have abolished it). Adjust the amount based on your state's slab.
Can I add custom salary components? ▼
Yes! Click "Add Earning Component" to add any custom component like Performance Bonus, Medical Allowance, Food Coupons, Leave Travel Allowance (LTA), etc. You can also remove any component you don't need.
